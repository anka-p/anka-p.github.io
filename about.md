        <h2>The Artist</h2>
        <p>Anka Priya is a multidisciplinary artist specializing in contemporary drawings and traditional handicrafts. With a focus on organic textures and intricate line work, her pieces explore the intersection of heritage techniques and modern aesthetics.</p>
        
        <p>Currently based in her home studio, she spends her time experimenting with clay glazes and charcoal studies.</p>

        <div class="details-list">
            <h3>Specialties</h3>
